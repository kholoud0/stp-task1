AboutUs

STP was founded in 2006 at Cairo university; The purpose of STP is to strengthen the leadership, skills,effectiveness and efficiency of involved people;

enabling them to further enrich the quality of the community and their personal life.

OBJECTIVE

  1. Reducing the gap between work sector & academic studies.
  2. Promoting the idea of development among youth.
  3. Contributing in the development & the awakening of the community.
  4. Convencing & supporting a dynamic learning community.

VISION

To be a Leading organization working on developing youth who will lead positive changes.

MISSION

To develop youth in various fields personally and technically through academic programs, projects, and events in a values oriented system, to take our part in providing a generation capable of playing its roles effictively towards itself, community and country.

CORE VALUES

Team spirit, Integrity, Leadership, and Excellence.
